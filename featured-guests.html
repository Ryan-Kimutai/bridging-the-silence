Tula Bischof

Licensed Psychologist & Founder, Loving To Love

Tula is a KCPB-registered psychologist based in Nairobi whose practice, Loving To Love, is built around decolonial and liberation-oriented work. She holds space for the systems, cultures, and relationships that shape people's lives, especially the ones that often get overlooked. For episode one, she turned the mic on Kiarie and got him talking about the breakdown, the silence before it, and why he started this show.
