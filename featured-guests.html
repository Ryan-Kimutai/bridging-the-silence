Tula Bischof

Licensed Psychologist

Tula is the founder of Loving To Love, a decolonial and liberation-oriented practice offering coaching, counselling, and consulting. Her work makes room for the systems, culture, and relationships that shape people’s lives, especially where identities and experiences are often overlooked. For the first conversation, Kiarie Wachira was the one answering the questions. Tula spoke with him about the breakdown, the silence before it, and why he started this show in the first place.
