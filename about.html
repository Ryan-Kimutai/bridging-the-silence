Medical Student Mental Health Advocate Host & Creator

Kiarie Wachira

I'm a medical student and mental health advocate, and most days the two aren't separate. I got into this after going through my own stretch of silence, the kind where you convince yourself you're fine until you're really not. Now I work with a team of psychiatrists, psychologists, and other advocates to make these conversations easier to have. Bridging the Silence is where that all comes together.

Why This Exists

Mental health conversations shouldn't be a privilege.

Mental health support shouldn't be hard to find. Too many of us were raised to treat silence as strength, especially around the things we're actually struggling with. So we tell our stories anyway, loud or quiet, because somewhere someone needs to hear them and say "me too". That's how a tribe gathers. Bridging the Silence is here to make it normal to talk, to ask for help, and to be heard
